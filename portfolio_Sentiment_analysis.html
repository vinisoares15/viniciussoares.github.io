The Influence of Social Networks on the Financial Market

  • Category: Academic Research
  • Where: University of Pernambuco
  • Project date: 01 August, 2021
  • Project Certificate: Link to Drive
  • Project Link: Link to Drive

Details

This article aims to analyze the behavior of the stock market in the Brazilian stock market based on the influence of social networks, especially Twitter, and determine this relationship of influence, as well as understand the market by the sentiment analysis using artificial intelligence. For this, Python programming language with libraries for analysis, Google Trends and the Twitter API were used. As results obtained in this research, it was possible to understand how social networks follow the herd instinct and how this directly influences the volume of operations and prices of stock market assets.
